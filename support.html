Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your device's settings. On iPhone: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > RepKit. On Android: Google Play Store > Subscriptions > RepKit.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple or Google depending on your device. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com (iOS) or play.google.com/store/account (Android) to submit a refund request.

How do I delete my account?

You can request account deletion by emailing support@repkitinc.com. We will process your request and delete your data within 30 days.

Is my data secure?

Yes. RepKit uses Google Firebase with encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and security rules that restrict access to authenticated users only. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What AI model does RepKit use?

RepKit's AI agent uses leading language models to power meeting prep, research, and productivity features. AI-generated content is for assistance only and should be verified independently.
